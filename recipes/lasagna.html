Description

A delicous Italian favorite sure to knock the pants off of any lasagana fan!

Perfect Lasagna by mistake

You wouldn't beleive it if it told you! My wife and I were sipping coffee when an old italian lady spilled her coffee while walking by on our table! "EXCUSIE" the old italian lady said. She said she was so sorry for having spilt the coffee. In return for our misfortune she would tell us about her infamous lasagna from old world Italy that was stolen from the famous Chef Boy-R-dee! It was taken from the vault inside of his ravioli castle! She decided to pass it on to us also because her life was near an end and she has no one else to pass the stolen secret on to anymore! In the following pages you will discover secret to Chef Boy-R-Dee's famous lasagna. It was so good that even he could not put it into a can.

INGREDIENTS

  • lasagna noodles
  • pasta sauce
  • ground beef
  • italian sausage
  • oregano
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozarella cheese
  • two cloves of garlic

STEPS

EST PREP TIME: 10 minutes EST COOK TIME: 90 minutes

  1. cook pasta in boiling water
  2. strain pasta set aside
  3. cook ground beef in medium pan until brown
  4. strain grease from pan and set ground beef aside in bowl
  5. cook italian sausage in same pan used for ground beef until cooked through
  6. strain grease from ground beef and set aside in same bowl as ground beef
  7. In medium sauce on med heat, add olive oil and smashed garlic cloves. Cook until fragrant
  8. Add pasta sauce and oregano, heat until simmering reduce heat to low and add ground beef and italian sausage into saucepan
  9. let sauce cook for 15 minutes and remove from heat
